# Privacy policy

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act (Loi Informatique et Libertés).

## Data controller

**JGoutin-dev SARL**

- Address: 45 rue Alain Decaux, 13100 Aix-en-Provence, France
- Email: [contact@jgoutin.dev](mailto:contact@jgoutin.dev)

## Data collected

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us via email correspondence:

- Name
- Email address
- Content of your message

We do not use analytics cookies, tracking cookies, or any form of automated data collection. The only local storage used is for theme preferences (light/dark mode), which is strictly necessary for the website's functionality.

## Purpose

Your data is collected for the following purposes:

- Responding to your professional inquiries
- Managing commercial relationships and prospect follow-up
- Fulfilling pre-contractual measures at your request

## Legal basis

The processing of your personal data is based on:

- **Legitimate interest** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)): for commercial prospect follow-up and responding to inquiries
- **Performance of pre-contractual measures or contractual obligations** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): when you request a quote or enter into a commercial relationship

## Data retention

- **Commercial prospects**: up to 3 years after the last meaningful contact
- **Contractual relationships**: for the duration of the contractual relationship and applicable legal retention periods

## Recipients

Your personal data is processed solely by JGoutin-dev SARL. We do not share your data with third parties, except:

- When required by law
- When necessary for hosting services (GitHub Pages, see below)

## Data hosting and transfers

This website is hosted by GitHub Pages in the United States. Data transfers outside the European Union are governed by:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
- The EU-US Data Privacy Framework adequacy decision (where applicable)

## Cookies

This website does not use cookies for analytics or tracking purposes. The only local storage used is for theme preferences (light/dark mode), which uses localStorage to remember your choice. This is a strictly necessary technical feature and does not constitute a cookie under GDPR definition.

## Your rights

Under GDPR and the French Data Protection Act, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access**: obtain confirmation of whether your data is processed and receive a copy
- **Right to rectification**: request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure**: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to restriction**: request limitation of processing
- **Right to data portability**: receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
- **Right to object**: object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Right to file a complaint**: file a complaint with the supervisory authority (CNIL in France)

To exercise your rights, contact us at: [contact@jgoutin.dev](mailto:contact@jgoutin.dev)

We will respond to your request within one month of receipt.

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or applicable regulations. The latest version will always be available on this page.
